Reth has been deployed as a StatefulSet.

To connect to JSON-RPC:
  kubectl port-forward svc/{{ include "reth.fullname" . }}-reth 8545:{{ .Values.reth.rpc.port }} -n {{ .Release.Namespace }}

To connect to the Engine API:
  kubectl port-forward svc/{{ include "reth.fullname" . }}-reth 8551:{{ .Values.reth.authrpc.port }} -n {{ .Release.Namespace }}

To view logs:
  kubectl logs -f sts/{{ include "reth.fullname" . }}-reth -n {{ .Release.Namespace }}
